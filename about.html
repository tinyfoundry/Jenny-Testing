Our mission

Family Fun Finder exists to make planning feel lighter for parents.

Calm, curated events

We collect public events and local submissions, then rewrite descriptions to be neutral, parent-focused, and easy to scan.

Two regions, one system

St. Augustine and Jacksonville share one curated list. Toggle by region to keep planning simple.

Human review before publish

Every new event is marked as pending. We manually review the details before adding it to the live list.

Curation policy

Our guidelines keep the list useful for busy families.
